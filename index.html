About Us


We offer foreign languages classes, multi-lingual translation services and interpreter services to special needs individuals across New York City. Our interpreter and translation services are a business-to-business service. We usually offer translation of internal documents to social service agencies, NFP’s, therapeutic practices, or medical offices across the city. These are the agencies that serve our students the most. Similarly, we offer interpreter services for special needs families looking to navigate social service agencies or governmental agencies across the city. Our interpreters serve as multi-lingual advocates and are generally free of charge to the family, but can be scheduled and paid for by the NFP itself. The language barrier could prevent NFP’s from securing non-anglophone clients. And, with our help they can increase customer retention and continue operating and seeking to improving the city we all know & love!
